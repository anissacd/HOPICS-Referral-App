How it works

Up and running in minutes

1

Sign in with Google

Use your existing HOPICS Google account — no new passwords to manage. Access is granted by your administrator.

2

Submit a referral

Search for a client by HMIS ID, fill in service category and urgency, and route to the appropriate program with one form.

3

Track & close the loop

Follow status updates, add case notes, log goals, and mark referrals complete — all synced back to Google Sheets.
